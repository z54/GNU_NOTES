hotkey | function
-|-
`C-x C-f` | open file
`C-x C-s` | save
`C-x C-c` | exit
`C-g` | cancel 
`C-x u` | undo

hotkey | function
-|-

`C-s` | search before
`C-r` | search after

---

hotkey | function
-|-
`M-w` | copy
`C-w` | cut
`C-y` | paste
`C-x h` | select all

### buffer

hotkey | function
-|-
`M-s` | new buffer
`C-x O` | switch buffer 
`C-x 3` | split the buffer to left and right
`C-x 2` | split the buffer to top and bottom
`C-x k` | close current buffer

### cursor

hotkey | function
-|-
`C-f` | Forward one character
`C-b` | Back one character
`M-f` | Forward one word
`M-b` | Back one word
`M-a` | Previous sentence
`M-e` | Next sentence
`C-n` | Next line
`C-p` | Previous line
`C-v` | Next screen
`M-v` |  Previous screen

---

M-1 M-0 C-n | Forward 10 lines

C-u 3 C-n | Forward 3 lines

C-u C-n | Forward 4 lines

C-u C-u C-n |  Forward 16 lines

C-a | Beginning of line

C-e | end of line
